We appreciate your interest in volunteering. Along with the warm fuzzies that many people receive from volunteering, this gig will also come with swag (individualized badges and a T-shirt) and an invite to the AWESOME after party. Please [fill out this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeW5u-gYEzOoOyZ9TWoMI8IXuq42bfeWB2GYmMtCq-3zDaooQ/viewform?pli=1) if you are interested or have questions about volunteering.

It is possible that volunteers will need to be shuffled around ahead of time. If this affects you, we will contact you in advance to determine your availability for needed positions.

Have fun doing what you love for a cause you can back! MAKE IT YOUR OWN!
