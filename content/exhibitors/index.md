title: Exhibitors
---
If you are interested in being an exhibitor at Makevention 2026, out exhibitor application will be available soon.

Booths are $10 if you are not selling items and $25 if you are selling items. Indoor and outdoor space is available as are over-21 spaces. The fee includes one table. Extra tables are available for $10 each.

Please pay your exhibitor fees after registration using the button below to pay with PayPal or a credit card. Booth fee waivers are available upon request.

Please check out our [Tips for Exhibitors](MakeventionExhibitorTipsSwitchyard.pdf).

Check out the current list of exhibitors for 2026: [Meet the Makers]({{< relref "meet-the-makers" >}})

If you still have questions, send us email at contact@makevention.org.
