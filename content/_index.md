# Makevention at Switchyard Park: October 10th, 2026

{{< video src="2024-Makevention.mp4" >}}

**Makevention 2026 will run from 10am through 4pm on Saturday October 10th 2026 at the [Switchyard Park Pavilion]({{< relref "map" >}}).** There will be demonstrations and all kinds of cool things happening all day. Kids will have a fun scavenger hunt to complete and there will be plenty of adult activities as well!

Due to the generosity of our [sponsors]({{< relref "sponsors" >}}), admission to Makevention 2026 is free! Donations are accepted, with a suggested donation amount of $5.

A list of our exhibitors may be found here: [Meet the Makers]({{< relref "meet-the-makers" >}})

If you’d like to sponsor Makevention 2026, visit our [Sponsorship page]({{< relref "sponsors" >}}) for more information.
